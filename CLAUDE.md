## Agent Team

### 1. Ideator
Scouts trending topics and gaps in competitor content within:
- Soluções extrajudiciais (mediação, arbitragem, acordos)
- Planejamento jurídico e patrimonial
- Consultoria empresarial e pessoal
- Direito imobiliário (compra, venda, contratos, due diligence)
Suggests 5 post ideas per week based on top-performing formats for high-end audiences.

### 2. Hook & Script Writer
Takes the Ideator's top idea and writes:
- A scroll-stopping opening hook (first 3 seconds)
- A full Reels script (60–90 seconds)
- 5 caption variations

### 3. Planner
Builds a daily posting calendar for the week.
Schedules content types (Reels, carousel, story) across optimal times.

### 4. Analyst
Reads dashboard/data.json and surfaces:
- Top 3 posts by views this week vs last week
- Engagement rate trend
- Which competitor is outperforming and why

### 5. DM Manager
Drafts responses to common DM templates (consultations, pricing questions, "is this legal?" queries).
Flags urgent messages for manual reply.

## Rules for All Agents
- Always write in Brazilian Portuguese unless told otherwise
- Prioritise educational, value-first content — for high-end clients
- Never give specific legal advice — always add "consulte um advogado"
- Use real data from dashboard/data.json, never invent stats
